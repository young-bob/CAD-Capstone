Why VSMS?

The 3 Gaps We Identified

Every volunteer and every organization faces these problems today.

🔍

Gap 1 — Hard to Find Opportunities

Volunteering information is scattered across dozens of websites, social media pages, and bulletin boards. The search cost is enormous — especially for students who need specific hours.

📋

Gap 2 — Manual Paperwork Chaos

Organizations manage volunteers using spreadsheets, paper sign-in sheets, and email threads. Coordinators are overwhelmed, and volunteers have a frustrating experience.

🚫

Gap 3 — No Proof, No Trust

It's easy to fake volunteer hours. There's no standardized verification system, and getting a certificate requires weeks of back-and-forth emails.

User Story 2 — The Coordinator

"Meet Mike — a coordinator at a food bank who manages 50 volunteers using spreadsheets and WhatsApp groups. He's drowning in admin work."

1

Posts a shift in seconds

Mike creates a new event with date, location, and required skills. It's live instantly — no more emailing volunteer lists. → Solves Gap 2

2

Volunteers find him automatically

VSMS's map and AI matching push his shift to nearby, qualified volunteers. Applications pour in without him lifting a finger. → Solves Gap 1

3

GPS-verified check-ins & instant attendance reports

Mike sees real-time GPS-verified attendance in his dashboard. One click exports a full attendance report — no manual counting. → Solves Gap 3

User Story 1 — The Volunteer

"Meet Sarah — a first-year PSW student who needs 120 volunteer hours to graduate. She doesn't know where to start."

1

Finds opportunities via Map & AI matching

Sarah opens VSMS, browses a live map of nearby shifts, and gets AI-powered recommendations based on her skills and schedule. → Solves Gap 1

2

Applies to a shift, tracks hours on dashboard

She taps "Apply", gets instant confirmation, and her dashboard updates with upcoming shifts and total hours logged. → Solves Gap 2

3

GPS check-in on site, generates certificate instantly

On the day of the event, Sarah checks in via GPS geofencing. After completing her shift, a verified certificate is generated immediately. → Solves Gap 3
